Sri Lankan Sloth Bear

Meet the Sri Lankan Sloth Bear, an endearing inhabitant of the island's dense forests. Recognized by its distinctive shaggy coat and distinctive white V-shaped mark on its chest, this charming creature captivates hearts with its unique characteristics. Armed with powerful claws, the Sloth Bear is an adept tree climber, effortlessly navigating the lush canopies in search of honey and fruits.

  • Distinctive shaggy coat and a white V-shaped mark on the chest
  • Adept tree climber, navigating canopies for honey and fruits; showcases tenacious courage when protecting cubs.

Sri Lankan Purple-Faced Langur

  • Unique purple fur on its face distinguishes it from other langur species
  • Arboreal lifestyle, primarily dwelling in the treetops; known for intricate social structures and harmonious group dynamics.
