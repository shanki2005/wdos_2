Wilpattu National Park

Experience the untouched wilderness and natural beauty of Wilpattu National Park. A haven for wildlife enthusiasts and nature lovers.

About Wilpattu National Park

Wilpattu, situated in the northwest of Sri Lanka, is the largest national park in the country. Its pristine landscapes and diverse habitats make it a sanctuary for a wide range of wildlife species.

  • Location: North Western Province, Sri Lanka
  • Main Attractions: Leopards, Elephants, Sloth Bears, and numerous bird species
