Yala National Park

Explore the diverse wildlife and natural beauty of Yala National Park. Discover the unique ecosystems and witness fascinating flora and fauna.

About Yala National Park

Yala National Park, located in the southeast of Sri Lanka, is renowned for its rich biodiversity. Spanning a vast area, the park is home to various ecosystems, including dense forests, grasslands, and coastal areas.

  • Location: Southern Province, Sri Lanka
  • Main Attractions: Leopard, Elephant, Sloth Bear, and a variety of bird species
