The Department of Wildlife Conservation, A Sustainable way forward


"The Sri Lankan Department of Wildlife Conservation preserves and sustains the nation's biodiversity. Committed to safeguarding flora and fauna, it enforces laws, collaborates for conservation, and promotes awareness. "

Main Conservations

Minneriya National Park

A picturesque park known for the gathering of elephants during the famous Minneriya Elephant Gathering.

Wilpattu National Park

Wilpattu, the largest national park in Sri Lanka, is renowned for its natural lakes and diverse fauna.

Horton Plains National Park

Discover the scenic beauty and unique flora and fauna of Horton Plains, a UNESCO World Heritage Site.
